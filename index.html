The Founder

The story of my journey.

Five years ago, I was in a car accident with no noticeable injuries. One morning in 2018 I woke up and couldn't move my hands.

A scan revealed serious damage to two cervical vertebrae. The choice: physiotherapy or surgery. That was the moment my recovery began.

In May 2018 I started clean eating and disciplined training. Months later, my condition was transformed.

In October 2018 I placed 2nd in my first Men's Physique competition, and 3rd the following year.

That experience taught me the value of nutrition. I founded Hi Pro Food and Powermax Vitamin Shop to bring the best to Ireland.

This is my journey. I hope you'll join me.
